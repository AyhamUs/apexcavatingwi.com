Who We Are

About
AP Excavating

AP Excavating LLC is a residential dirt work company based out of southeastern Wisconsin — we also bring forth our expertise in central Wisconsin. We provide a variety of services including grading, drainage solutions, trenching/utility work, land clearing/tree work, as well as demolition and concrete.

We offer the best services in the industry with the help of great employees, top notch equipment, and high-quality materials.

What We Do

Residential Excavation Services

Grading

Your project deserves a grading team that understands the importance of doing it right the first time. We take the time to evaluate your land, listen to your goals, and provide honest recommendations that protect your investment. From leveling and slope correction to drainage solutions and site prep, we deliver dependable results with clear communication and a commitment to quality. When you work with us, you get a partner who treats your property like their own.

Land Clearing & Tree Work

When it comes to land clearing and tree work, precision matters. Our team delivers safe, efficient, and environmentally responsible solutions for residential and agricultural properties. Whether you need full-site clearing, selective tree removal, storm cleanup, or routine maintenance, we bring the equipment, expertise, and attention to detail that get the job done right the first time. We don't just clear land — we create clean, build-ready spaces that set your project up for success.

Concrete

We handle small residential concrete projects with the same care and attention to detail we'd want on our own property. From decorative patios to safe, level walkways and sturdy pads, we take the time to understand your goals and deliver a finished product you'll be proud of. Honest recommendations, dependable service, and craftsmanship you can trust — that's our promise on every job.

Demolition

When it's time to remove a structure, you need a crew that can get it done quickly, safely, and with precision. Our residential demolition services cover everything from small buildings and garages to concrete removal and full site cleanup. We combine skilled operators with the right equipment to deliver fast turnaround, minimal disruption, and a spotless finish. We don't just tear things down — we clear the way for your next project to begin.

Drainage Solutions

Standing water and poor drainage can cause serious damage to your property over time. We design and install effective drainage solutions — including French drains, swales, and regrading — that protect your yard, foundation, and landscaping for the long term.

Trenching & Utility Work

From utility lines to irrigation systems, our trenching services deliver clean, precise excavation for residential projects of all sizes. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your property while ensuring every trench meets code and is ready for the next phase of your project.
